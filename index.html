What ?? Jedi and Sith. What is Happening in Here

Jedi and sith are not enemies in real life. They are just two ways of thinking with two different philosophical perspectives. On a very basic level, this is my conceptualization of the Sith path, but rather than being external (like your examples of technology etc.) it is entirely internal.

Not everything I do is for myself, because most of the time I do things for others, like friends and family, but what I do in my own time is accounted for. Since this is the only life I have, I try not to waste it.

In addition, a big part of the Sith path is integrating the ugly parts of your Self into the whole. We do not ignore the dark, shadowy parts of our emotions and thoughts. Rather, we accept and embrace them. Knowing what we are capable of makes it much easier to understand ourselves and the world. Jedi take a different approach. They choose to push these thoughts and feelings out in favor of healthier ones. Which is equally valuable. The important thing is that whatever the path, it must always remain within healthy boundaries. All paths can have overzealous followers.

I think the Force is more than light and dark, there is not just black and white. I am both sith and Jedi and balanced.
